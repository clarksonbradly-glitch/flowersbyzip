FlowersByZip was born from years of watching local flower shops pour their hearts into their craft — knowing that a direct order means more revenue stays with them, their staff, and their community.

We believe that ordering directly from a local florist is the most beneficial way for everyone — customers get a personal experience and florists maximize the value of every order. This directory exists to make that connection as simple as a ZIP code.

Every shop listed here is intended to be a local flower shop — real florists in real communities.

Are you a local flower shop?
