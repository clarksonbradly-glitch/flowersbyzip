Built by a florist,
for florists — and the
people who love them.

FlowersByZip was born from years of watching independent flower shops pour their hearts into their craft — only to have wire services take a massive cut of every order through fees, commissions, and mandatory purchases.

We believe customers deserve to know where their money actually goes. And we believe florists deserve to keep what they earn. This directory exists to make that connection as simple as a ZIP code.

Every shop listed here is independently verified. No chains, no franchise operations, no wire-service-owned storefronts. Just real florists in real communities.
